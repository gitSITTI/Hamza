Real Estate Advanced Investor

Multi-property portfolio, leveraged growth, refinance cycling
Questions to ask
  • How many investment properties do you currently own? New field
  • What is the total current market value of your rental portfolio? New field
  • What is your average gross monthly rent across all units? New field
  • What is your target cash-on-cash return for new acquisitions? New field
  • At what combined LTV do you plan to stop leveraging? New field
  • Do you plan to do a 1031 exchange when you sell, or pay capital gains? New field
  • At what age do you want to start liquidating the portfolio? New field
Calculations to add
  • Portfolio net operating income (NOI) by year with vacancy and expense growth Calc
  • Cash-on-cash return = annual cash flow ÷ total cash invested Calc
  • Debt-service coverage ratio (DSCR) per property and portfolio-wide Calc
  • Equity growth: appreciation + principal paydown compounded annually Calc
  • Refinance trigger: cash-out available when LTV drops below your target Calc
  • Tax drag: depreciation recapture and capital gains at liquidation age Calc
Dashboard metrics to show
  • Portfolio equity timeline vs. projected liquidation value Data
  • Annual cash flow waterfall: gross rent → vacancy → expenses → debt service → net Data
  • Milestone table: DSCR ≥ 1.25 years, max leverage year, full pay-off age Data

Regular Homeowner

Primary residence equity, mortgage payoff, home improvement ROI
Questions to ask
  • What is your home's current estimated market value? New field
  • What is your remaining mortgage balance? New field
  • What is your mortgage interest rate and original term? New field
  • Do you plan to make extra principal payments, or keep to the schedule? New field
  • Are you planning a major renovation or addition? If so, at what year and cost? New field
  • Do you expect to sell and downsize, upsize, or stay indefinitely? New field
  • Are you paying PMI? If so, at what LTV do you plan to remove it? New field
Calculations to add
  • Home equity by year = appreciated value − remaining mortgage balance Calc
  • PMI removal year: when LTV crosses 80% based on payments + appreciation Calc
  • Extra payment scenario: payoff date and total interest saved Calc
  • Sell vs. stay: net proceeds after agent fees, taxes, and moving costs Calc
  • Renovation ROI: estimated added value vs. out-of-pocket cost Calc
Dashboard metrics to show
  • Home equity as share of total net worth over time Data
  • Amortization chart: principal balance, interest paid, and equity by year Data
  • Break-even year for extra payments vs. investing the same cash in VOO Data

Markets Investor

Crypto · Stocks / ETFs · Options · Futures · Commodities
Questions to ask — per asset class
  • Crypto: Current balance and average annual return assumption (%) New field
  • Alt stocks / sector ETFs: Balance and expected annual return beyond your core VOO position New field
  • Options: Estimated average annual P&L (can be negative) from premium writing or directional trades New field
  • Futures / commodities: Account balance and average annual return or P&L New field
  • Which markets are active for you? (multi-select: crypto, stocks, options, futures, commodities) New field
  • What % of new annual surplus do you allocate to each market? New field
  • Do you plan to treat markets gains as re-investable or as spending income? New field
Calculations to add
  • Each market's balance projected annually: balance × (1 + return)^years + contributions Calc
  • Options / futures annual P&L added to economic gross income each year Calc
  • Combined alt-markets net worth: crypto + alt stocks + commodities + futures Calc
  • Short-term vs. long-term capital gains tax on market positions at sale Calc
  • Stress scenario: crypto crash −60%, options blow-up year → net worth impact Calc
Dashboard metrics to show
  • Stacked area chart: VOO / crypto / alt-stocks / commodities balance over time Data
  • Market allocation pie: % of investable assets per market today vs. at retirement Data
  • Annual P&L table: options + futures income contribution by year Data

Blended Family / Child Support

Remarried with prior-relationship children, child support obligations
Questions to ask
  • Are you paying or receiving child support? If paying, monthly amount? New field
  • How many children from a prior relationship? Ages? New field
  • At what age does your child support obligation end per court order? New field
  • Are you contributing to college savings (529) for prior children? Monthly amount? New field
  • Do you share health insurance costs for prior children with your ex? New field
  • How many children in your current household? Do you anticipate more? New field
  • Do you have a prenuptial or post-nuptial agreement that affects asset ownership? New field
  • Are there any shared debt obligations from the prior marriage (joint loans, HELOC)? New field
Calculations to add
  • Child support annual drag: monthly × 12, ends at court-ordered age cutoff year Calc
  • Post-child-support surplus boost: cash freed up when obligation ends Calc
  • 529 / college savings accumulation vs. estimated cost at each child's age 18 Calc
  • Blended household childcare cost: current children + prior children's custody days Calc
  • Net worth sensitivity: how much does ending child support in Y years change the 20-year projection? Calc
Dashboard metrics to show
  • Child support obligation timeline: monthly cost by year with end date marker Data
  • Before/after child support end: net worth at retirement under both scenarios Data
  • College funding gap: 529 projected balance vs. estimated full cost at age 18 Data
